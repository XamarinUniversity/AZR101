AZR101 Introduction to Azure

Exercise 1: Create an Azure account

This exercise walks you through the steps for registering and creating a new Microsoft Azure account. If you already have access to an Azure account, you can skip this exercise.

Open the registration page

  1. Open a web browser and point it at Microsoft Azure Portal.
  2. Click the Start Free button, or the Free Account link at the top of the page.
  3. On the regional account creation page, click the Start Now button.

Log in to your Microsoft account

  1. Log in to your Microsoft account (e.g. MSDN account, Live account, etc.). If you don't currently have an account, you can click the Sign Up Now link on the page.
  2. Once you have authenticated your account, you can step through the sign-up process.Â 
  3. Once the account has been created, you will get the welcome/setup screen.
  4. Click the Start Free button to go to the Azure Management Portal.
Note: The information collected depends on what country you are in. Azure will collect a credit card, but this is strictly for identity verification purposes only. The card will not be charged and your free account will not be upgraded to a full subscription unless you specifically request it.

Exercise summary

Congratulations! In this exercise, you registered for a new trial and/or created a new account to give you access to Azure.
